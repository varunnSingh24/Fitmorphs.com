The Clinical Method

The FitMorphs Methodology

Seven years of clinical practice. Ten thousand patients. One protocol — codified, lab-monitored, evidence-anchored. This is how it actually works.

Architecture

Six Pillars. One Ecosystem.

I

Precision Nutrition

Scientifically calibrated macros tailored to the patient’s Indian palate, allergies, and lab profile. No standard template — every plan is composed from the patient's CGM curves and lipid panel.

II

Metabolic Reset

Targeted physiological interventions (sleep window, post-meal walks, fasted training, polyphenol density) that restore insulin sensitivity at the cellular level.

III

Behavioural Coaching

Weekly habit architecture sessions, accountability scaffolding, and psychological tooling that turn a 90-day protocol into a permanent operating system.

IV

Real-Time Tracking

Continuous glucose monitoring (optional), daily glucose & sleep logs, and a private clinical dashboard that surfaces actionable patterns to the doctor.

V

Peer Community

A vetted private network of current patients walking the same road. Accountability, lived expertise, and on-demand support outside clinical hours.

VI

Doctor Supervision

Every protocol decision medically reviewed. Medications are tapered only as lab values respond — never on a fixed schedule, never without consent.

Lab Cadence

What We Measure. And When.

Baseline

Day 0 — Full metabolic workup

HbA1c, fasting glucose, fasting insulin, HOMA-IR, full lipid panel, liver function, kidney function, TSH, vitamin D, B12, ferritin, body composition. CGM placement (optional). Clinical screening call & goal alignment.

Week 4

Fasting glucose + body composition recheck

Early-signal lab to confirm trajectory. CGM data reviewed weekly between formal checkpoints. Protocol adjustments made on the basis of trend, not absolute readings.

Week 8

HbA1c progress check + medication review

First HbA1c re-test (a 60-day signal is informative even though the technical half-life of HbA1c is 90 days). Medication review with the patient's treating physician if values warrant a taper.

Week 12

Full metabolic re-workup

Repeat of the Day-0 panel. The clinical lead writes a summary report and a forward-looking maintenance protocol. Outcome data is logged to our aggregate cohort statistics.

Week 16+

Maintenance & relapse-prevention

Quarterly HbA1c + lipid checks for the first year post-program. Lifetime patient-portal access. Refresher calls available on request.

Safety Architecture

Nothing Is Left to Chance.

Medication safety. No patient is ever asked to reduce, modify, or stop medication based on self-monitoring alone. All medication changes are made in writing, by our medical lead, in consultation with the patient's treating physician, with lab values confirming the change is safe.

Clinical eligibility. We do not accept every applicant. Patients in active emergency treatment, pregnant or nursing women, individuals under 18, and patients with certain endocrine pathologies are referred to specialist care. This is why our acceptance rate is published lower than industry, but our completion rate is higher.

Outcome guarantee. If your HbA1c hasn’t measurably improved within 60 days of starting your protocol — while following our team's guidance — we refund your full program fee. See the refund policy.
