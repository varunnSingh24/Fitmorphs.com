Yes. Clinical studies and our own results show that by implementing targeted nutritional and lifestyle changes, the body can regain insulin sensitivity and beta cell function, allowing blood sugar to normalize without medication.

Not at all. We integrate our protocols with your regular Indian diet. We teach you how to portion, pair, and time your meals intelligently so you don't have to give up the foods you love.

Do I need to stop my current medications immediately? +

No. You continue your medications initially. Our medical experts will coordinate with you to gradually taper them off as your blood sugar levels naturally stabilize.

Is this a starvation diet? +

Absolutely not. Starvation diets damage your metabolism. You will eat nutrient-dense, satisfying meals constructed to heal your metabolic engine.
