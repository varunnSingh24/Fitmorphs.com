The Doctrine

Three Principles We Won't Trade On.

I

Doctor-led, always.

Every protocol is designed, monitored, and adjusted by MBBS-qualified physicians. No app-only coaching. No "wellness influencer" guesswork. Real medical eyes on real lab values.

II

Data, not dogma.

HbA1c, fasting glucose, lipids, liver markers, body composition — every protocol decision is anchored in your lab values, not in templated diet plans. Your physiology writes the prescription.

III

Reversal, not management.

Indian medicine treats metabolic disease as a life sentence. We don't. The body is engineered to repair — given the right inputs, supervised correctly, in time. We measure success in medications safely tapered.

A Quiet Practice, Growing

Seven Years. One Doctrine.

  1. 2018

    The first ten patients.

    Dr. Mane begins running a small clinical experiment with ten Pune-based Type 2 patients. Within 90 days, seven are off Metformin. The proof-of-concept is enough to commit.

  2. 2020

    The protocol is codified.

    Three years of patient outcomes converge into the six-pillar reversal protocol. Dr. Richa Mane joins as Lead Nutritionist. The practice formalises its lab-monitoring workflow.

  3. 2022

    1,000th reversal.

    The thousandth medically-verified diabetes reversal is logged. Two endocrinologists join the medical board. Times of India publishes the first feature on the practice.

  4. 2024

    The 60-day HbA1c guarantee.

    Seven years of outcome data become a public commitment: if your HbA1c hasn't moved in 60 days, your fee is refunded. No clauses.

  5. 2026

    10,000th life transformed.

    45,000+ combined kilograms lost. 2.8 average HbA1c points reduced. Three new pathway protocols enter clinical trial: PCOD reset, liver reversal, and lean-mass recomposition.
