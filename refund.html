Refund Policy

A doctor-led program is only as honest as its guarantee.

Last updated May 2026 · v1.0

The 60-Day HbA1c Guarantee. If your HbA1c hasn't measurably improved within 60 days of starting your protocol — while following our team's guidance and attending all scheduled clinical reviews — we refund your full program fee. No questions, no clauses.

1. Who This Applies To

The 60-Day HbA1c Guarantee covers patients enrolled in our flagship Diabetes Reversal and HbA1c Management programs with a baseline HbA1c of 5.7% or higher (i.e. pre-diabetic or diabetic range). Patients in other programs (e.g. fat-loss recomposition, PCOD reset, lean-mass) are covered by program-specific guarantees described in their welcome packet.

2. Eligibility Conditions

  • You must submit a baseline HbA1c lab report (taken within 14 days of enrolment) and a follow-up HbA1c report at the 60-day mark, both from an NABL-accredited lab.
  • You must complete at least 80% of scheduled clinical check-ins during the 60-day window.
  • You must follow the personalised nutrition, sleep, and movement guidance issued by your clinical team. Significant unreported deviation from the protocol disqualifies the guarantee.
  • "Measurable improvement" is defined as any reduction in HbA1c value from baseline (e.g. 8.4% → 8.2% qualifies as measurable).

3. How to Request a Refund

Email hello@fitmorphs.com with the subject "Refund Request — 60-Day Guarantee" and attach:

  • Your baseline and 60-day HbA1c lab reports (PDF).
  • Your patient ID and program enrolment date.

Our clinical lead reviews your file within 7 working days. If the guarantee is honoured, refunds are processed within 14 working days via the original payment method.

4. Standard Cancellation Window

Outside the outcome guarantee, you may cancel within 7 days of payment for any reason and receive a 100% refund minus any payment-processor fees, provided you have not yet attended your first clinical onboarding call. After the onboarding call, refund eligibility is governed by the outcome guarantee above and the program completion percentage.

5. Partial Refunds & Medical Pause

If a medical condition unrelated to our program requires you to pause (e.g. surgery, pregnancy, family emergency), we will pause your program at no cost for up to 90 days. If you must withdraw, a prorated refund based on unused program weeks is issued at our discretion. Contact your clinical team to initiate a pause or partial-refund discussion.

What is not refundable. Lab test fees you've paid directly to third-party labs, third-party device fees (CGM sensors, smart scales), and any sessions with external specialists that we've facilitated on your behalf. We refund only what you have paid directly to FitMorphs.

6. Disputes

If we cannot reach a resolution through the refund process above, the dispute is governed by our Terms of Service, with jurisdiction in Pune, Maharashtra.
