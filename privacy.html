Privacy Policy

Last updated May 2026 · v1.0

FitMorphs Health Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("FitMorphs", "we", "our") treats your personal and clinical data as a privileged trust. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, who can see it, and the choices you have at every step.

1. Data We Collect

When you apply, enrol, or use our website, we may collect:

  • Identification: name, age, sex, city, phone, email.
  • Clinical inputs: condition history, current medications, lab values (HbA1c, fasting glucose, lipids, liver markers), goals, allergies, and dietary preferences.
  • Behavioural & engagement: sleep, activity, food logs, and CGM traces if you opt into device sync.
  • Technical: IP, device, browser, and aggregate analytics (Meta Pixel, Google Analytics) for marketing performance.

2. How We Use It

  • To design and adapt your clinical protocol: this is the core of our service.
  • To communicate with you across WhatsApp, email, and phone calls (only contacts you initiate or consent to).
  • To monitor outcomes, fine-tune our protocols, and publish aggregated, de-identified statistics in our marketing.
  • To comply with applicable Indian medical-records, tax, and consumer-protection regulations.

3. Who Can See Your Data

Your individual clinical record is accessible only to (a) you, (b) your assigned doctor and clinical coach, and (c) authorised FitMorphs medical-records administrators. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties under any circumstances. We may share data with:

  • Lab partners you choose for test bookings, only for the limited purpose of completing the test.
  • Payment processors (Razorpay, Stripe), strictly for transaction processing.
  • WhatsApp Business API for our messaging channel.
  • Email infrastructure (Postmark / SendGrid) for transactional and marketing emails.
  • Aggregated analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel) using de-identified data only.

Your rights. You may request access to, correction of, or permanent deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@fitmorphs.com with the subject "Data Request". We respond within 7 working days. After program completion, your clinical record is retained for 7 years per Indian medical-records guidance unless you specifically request earlier deletion.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for essential session management, performance analytics, and conversion tracking. You can disable non-essential cookies through your browser settings without losing core functionality.

5. Data Security

Personal and clinical data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Access is restricted via role-based controls and audited every quarter. Despite reasonable safeguards, no transmission over the internet is ever 100% secure. In the event of a breach affecting you, we will notify you within 72 hours.

6. Children's Privacy

Our programs are not designed for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Updates to This Policy

We will update this policy from time to time. Material changes are communicated to enrolled patients via email. The "Last updated" date above always reflects the most recent revision.
